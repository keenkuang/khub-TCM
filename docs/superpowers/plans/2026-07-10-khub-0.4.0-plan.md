## 4 个模块实现（将计划中的代码写入对应文件）

### analysis.py
- `build_syndrome_formula_matrix(store) -> dict`
- `analyze_constitution_evolution(store, pid) -> dict`

### visualize.py
- `get_health_trends(store, pid) -> dict`
- `_infer_constitution(records, evolution) -> dict`

### diagnosis.py
- `_SYNDROME_FORMULA_MAP` 14 种证型→方剂知识库
- `suggest_formula(syndrome, provider=None) -> list[dict]`
- `check_incompatibility(formulas) -> list[str]`

### tracking.py
- `evaluate_efficacy(store, pid) -> dict`

### api.py 端点
- `GET /clinical/analysis/{pid}/matrix`
- `GET /clinical/analysis/{pid}/evolution`
- `GET /clinical/tracking/{pid}`
- `GET /clinical/trends/{pid}`
- `POST /clinical/diagnosis/suggest`

### cli.py 子命令
- `khub clinical matrix <pid>`
- `khub clinical trends <pid>`
- `khub clinical suggest <syndrome>`
- `khub clinical tracking <pid>`
